About me

Who I am.

I'm Langelihle "Langa" Malaza, a B. Computer Information Systems (Honours) graduate with a strong interest in software engineering, XR simulation, and business analysis. I enjoy building interactive systems that solve real problems — from tractor driving simulators for agricultural training to AI-assisted tools for personal finance.

Interests & Focus Areas

  • XR: VR, AR, and MR for training and education.
  • Embedded systems and Arduino-based prototypes connecting software to hardware.
  • Business analysis and process mapping to align solutions with how organisations work.
  • Data and analytics as feedback loops for improving systems and UX.
  • Personal finance and community-banking tools for financial literacy and access.

How I Work

I see software engineering as a tool to solve problems, not just a way to ship features. I try to add value by keeping focus on impact and clarity.

Hardworking & consistent Steady progress, clear milestones, reliable delivery.
Creative problem solver Exploring different interaction modes before committing to a solution.
User-centred Focused on how people experience a system, not just how it works internally.
Structure-focused Clean architecture, maintainable code, documentation future developers can follow.
