About

Profile

I am Langelihle "Langa" Malaza, a B. Computer Information Systems (Honours) graduate with a strong interest in software engineering, XR simulation, and business analysis. I enjoy building interactive systems that solve real problems, from tractor driving simulators for agricultural training to AI-assisted tools for personal finance.

Interests & Focus Areas

  • XR: VR, AR, and MR for training and education.
  • Embedded systems and Arduino-based prototypes that connect software to hardware.
  • Business analysis and process mapping to align solutions with how organisations actually work.
  • Data and analytics as feedback loops for improving systems and user experience.
  • Personal finance and community-banking tools that support financial literacy and access.

How I Work

I see software engineering as a tool to solve problems, not just a way to ship features. I try to add value to the organisations and teams I work with by keeping the focus on impact and clarity.

  • Hardworking and consistent: I prefer steady progress, clear milestones, and reliable delivery.
  • Creative problem solver: I enjoy exploring different interaction modes and designs before choosing a solution.
  • User-centred: I care about how people experience a system, not just how it works internally.
  • Structure-focused: I like clean architecture, maintainable code, and documentation that future developers can follow.
