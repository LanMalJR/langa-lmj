Tractor Driving VR

I built a VR tractor simulator for safety training of novice agricultural and engineering students. Features included realistic dynamics, seat-gated controls, and startup/shutdown procedures. Completed in under 8 months as an Honours project.

Supervisor: Dr B. Botha Co-assessor: Mr C. Cilliers Tools: C#, Unity, Blender, XRI, Git Outputs: Prototype, Technical manual, User manual
VRUnityC#XRI

Eye-Tracking Study (Tobii)

Analysed gaze behaviour using a Tobii device as part of the HCI-Honours module. The study included seven participants and reported heat maps, fixations, and related metrics.

Supervisor: Prof. T. Stott Assessor: Dr B. Botha Team: 3 · Participants: 7 Stack: Python, Excel, Tobii tooling Output: Research Paper
HCIAnalyticsTobii
