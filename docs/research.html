Tractor Driving VR

I built a VR tractor simulator for safety training of novice agricultural and engineering students. Features include realistic dynamics, seat-gated controls, and startup/shutdown procedures. Completed in under 8 months as an Honours project.

  • Supervisor: Dr B. Botha
  • Co-assessor: Mr C. Cilliers
  • Tools & languages: C#, Unity, Blender, XR Interaction Toolkit, Git
  • Outputs: Prototype, Technical manual, User manual
VRUnityC#XRI

Eye-tracking Study (Tobii)

I analysed gaze behaviour using a Tobii device as part of the HCI-Honours module. The study included seven participants and reported heat maps, fixations, and related metrics.

  • Supervisor: Prof. T. Stott
  • Assessor: Dr B. Botha
  • Team size: 3
  • Participants: 7
  • Methods: Heat maps, fixation analysis
  • Stack: Python, Excel, Tobii tooling
  • Outputs: Research Paper

Business Analysis for ACCESS

  • Assessor: Dr E. E. Benedict
  • Team size: 6
  • Focus: NPO registration pathway, Business Model Canvas, Stakeholder Overview
  • Outputs: Business analysis report and slides

  • Assessor: Dr R. Bezuidenhout
